About St. Mary's Hospital

St. Mary's Hospital was founded in 1992 to serve the growing healthcare needs of Greenfield City and surrounding communities. Over the years we have grown from a small clinic into a full-service hospital with a focus on patient-centred care, modern diagnostics, and community outreach.
